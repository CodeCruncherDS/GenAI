## Situation
At Deloitte USI, clients faced fragmented ML delivery practices that lacked formal governance, reproducibility, and operational transparency, leading to inconsistent deployments and support challenges.

## Approach
- Established MLOps governance frameworks incorporating MLflow, feature stores, and standardized deployment patterns.
- Collaborated with cross-discipline teams to translate business objectives into scalable, deployable ML architectures.
- Led knowledge transfer and mentoring to embed best practices for pipeline testing, CI/CD hygiene, and monitoring.

## Outcomes
Delivered production-grade ML solutions with repeatable pipelines, improved auditability, and enhanced team readiness for ongoing model operations and scaling.
